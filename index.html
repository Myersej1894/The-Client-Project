About Us

Hello, my name is Faye and this is my traveling floral shop that sells flower arrangements and succulents.
This RV-turned-greenhouse is where all the plants are grown and anyone can pick the style and vibe they want their arrangement to be,
which are all created on the road. Flowers from Faye is environmentally conscious, so all our products are organic and recyclable.
We hope you let your creativity run free so we can make your dream arrangement come to life!
